**Raju Kankipati:** The other demo we have here at OFC 2026 in LA is the POET Blazar, which is an external cavity laser which uses the POET Optical Interposer as the base platform.

As the industry is seeing this tremendous growth in external light source demand, so what POET brings to this market and the industry is the differentiation utilizing interposer as the base platform. So the external cavity laser, instead of a traditional DFB laser, we actually use a gain material still based on indium phosphide but much easier to make and much easier to yield.

And we use the interposer to select the wavelengths, and we are actually demonstrating a very high power. Here we can see close to 18 dBm, but actually our goal is to get beyond 20 dBm of output power this year.

And this product we expect to have significant impact in the overall industry as we scale into high volume production.
