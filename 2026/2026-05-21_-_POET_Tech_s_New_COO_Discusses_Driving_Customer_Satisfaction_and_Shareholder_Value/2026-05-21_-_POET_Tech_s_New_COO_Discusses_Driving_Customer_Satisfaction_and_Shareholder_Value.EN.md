#  POET Tech's New COO Discusses Driving Customer Satisfaction and Shareholder Value 

2026-05-21

- Video: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0IFtL-phVc>

**Sandeep Kumar:** I see an opportunity to build an organization that can scale. I believe what technology and Team POET has will speed the industry's move towards photonics integration. And I want to be part of that story. Suresh has had this vision for many years. I am confident that his leadership and this technology will take this company to a different level, serving the AI infrastructure and AI network connectivity businesses.

As the Chief Operating Officer, my role is to make sure our teams and our business scales. And we exceed the expectations of our customers. I always believed in differentiating and ruthless execution in everything we do. The differentiation is inherent in the Optical Interposer technology. Successful execution will result in customer satisfaction. And I have absolute conviction that we can do these things. And as a result, we will further establish our credibility in the industry. And we will see POET's valuation multiply.

Simply put, I want to improve shareholder value. And we will do that by hitting the customer delivery milestones. We want to deliver on time, improve our predictability, and become a reliable supplier to our customers. It is also important that our employees feel fulfilled and understand how vital their work is.

Rarely in my career have I seen the kind of potential for high-volume growth as POET has right now.

POET has expanded our manufacturing in fabrication and assembly facilities here in Malaysia. And there are several reasons for that expansion. One is to meet the anticipated demand for our external light sources and Optical Engine products. We have built capacity to produce one million units per year.

Inside the operation centers here in Penang and nearby NationGate, we have deployed equipment, including deposition, etching, advanced packaging tools to do high-accuracy assembly. We are also benefiting from the availability of good talent in the area. We continue to hire experienced engineers who can operate this equipment and solve problems. That acquisition of talent and quality equipment is essential to deliver our products to our AI customers.

Over the last few quarters, POET has seen extraordinary growth in customer interest. There is growing demand for our products. POET's disruptive Optical Interposer platform technology delivers lower cost, consumes less power, is smaller in size, and built for high-volume manufacturing.
